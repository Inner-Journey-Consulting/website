Welcome

We're a team of experienced social workers, therapists, and youth support specialists working with individuals, couples, families, and young people across the region.

Our work is depth-oriented and Jungian-informed — grounded in analytical psychology's attention to the unconscious, to dream and symbol, and to the lifelong process of individuation. We don't just treat symptoms; we help you understand what they have to tell you about who you are and who you're becoming, in connection with others, with yourself, and with the living world.

Ways into the work

Depth & Jungian Psychotherapy

Working with the deeper currents beneath everyday struggle — through Jungian and analytical approaches, dreamwork, and focusing — toward self-understanding and a steadier sense of ground.

Youth, Peer & Family Support

Team-based care for young people facing serious mental-health and behavioral challenges, with family engagement and psycho-education woven throughout, grounded in early, community-rooted intervention.

Youth / Peer / Family

Somatic & Trauma Therapy

Gently helping the nervous system move out of survival mode, using EMDR, brainspotting, sensory-motor work, and trauma-informed yoga.
